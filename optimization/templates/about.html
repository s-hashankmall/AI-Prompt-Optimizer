About This Application

Welcome to the Prompt Optimizer! This application leverages the power of AI to help users generate and optimize prompts for better responses from language models.

Features

  • Prompt Optimization: Get tailored prompts that enhance clarity and specificity.
  • AI-Driven Responses: Receive informative answers based on your original and optimized prompts.
  • User Ratings: Rate the responses to help improve the quality of generated answers.
  • Intuitive Interface: Enjoy a user-friendly design that makes prompt optimization easy and efficient.

How It Works

Simply enter your prompt, and the application will provide optimized versions tailored for clarity and effectiveness. You can then select one of the optimized prompts to receive an answer generated by the AI.
